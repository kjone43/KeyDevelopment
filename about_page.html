About Key

How did I get into web development and communications?

While my background was originally in Exercise Science, not satisfied with the field and its prospects – I was in search of a change. During COVID, my interest in programming developed and prompted me to create a website that could serve as my portfolio and potentially a business opportunity. Excited by the creativity and freedom programming gave me, thus my interest in communications was sparked.

Future of Key Development?

I plan to provide organizations with updates to their websites. Or perhaps provide new businesses with websites and/or social media help? Honestly, you ask and I’ll see what I can do!
